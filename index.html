Research Snapshot

+

You have the hard part already: real authority as an operator who owns and runs studios, strong content, and a clear program for studio owners in The Geronimo Academy. Attention is not your problem. Capturing it is.

The wedge: your entire front door is a cold "Apply Now" form straight off the bio. That only converts the few owners already sold. Every interested owner who is not ready to apply today hits a wall, with nothing lower-commitment to take, no email captured, and no way for you to follow up.

The play: put a low-friction lead magnet in front of the application. A self-scoring Studio Owner's Scorecard on the five systems that let a studio run without its owner, an opt-in that builds your list, and a nurture sequence that warms owners from a cold reel into a booked call.

One honest note: the build keeps your advertised metrics and testimonials off the public copy and leans on what is plainly true, that you own and run studios and coach owners. No invented proof, and no income or revenue claims anywhere.

The Lead Magnet: The Studio Owner's Scorecard

+

The Scorecard is the new front door. It is your exact positioning, "build a studio that runs without you," made low-friction so owners who are not ready to apply still raise their hand. Three parts, one funnel:

Primary: The Studio Owner's ScorecardA free, self-scored assessment across five systems: lead generation, sales, retention, team, and owner time. Owners find the one system leaking and the one to fix first.
Built to capture it: The Opt-In FunnelA landing page in your brand that trades the Scorecard for a name and email, turning anonymous traffic into a list you own.
Built to convert it: The Nurture SequenceFive emails that deliver real value, build trust from your operator credibility, and route warmed owners into a booked call or your application.

The application still exists at the end. The difference is it now receives warm, educated owners instead of cold clicks.
